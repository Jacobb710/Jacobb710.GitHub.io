About Us

Founded in 1972 by a group of 3 best friends who all had a passion for music, we started off in our basements at first just fixing guitars and pianos for people. We have been serving our customers with a passion for music for decades now. We strive on our amazing customer service abilities and a legacy spanning over 5 decades. GAP music was created to make sure that music could thrive, and through the numerous years we have learned more about musicsians needs and how to proivde the best of the best quality to our customers. We have labs across the country currently working on and testing the newest materials to upgrade and to make your instruments the best that they can possibly be.
